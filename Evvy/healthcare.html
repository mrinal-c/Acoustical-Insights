Sound Power Measurements for Industrial Equipment - Union City, CA

This project involves the quantification of sound levels from industrial equipment. A challenge for this project included high noise levels at the evaluation site. The site is located in the parking lot adjacent to a manufacturing facility. Additionally, neighboring manufactures received regular delivery shipments, aircraft were regularly seen overhead, and a train line was approximately 500 ft from the test location. RSG acted as a prime contractor interacting the client and conducting the analysis and modeling. Coffman supporting the project by conducting measurements. The measurement 3 COFFMAN ENGINEERS Barnobi Resume protocol required addressing the high background noise levels from these various sources. RSG identified a sound intensity measurement as an appropriate method that reduced the impacts of the environmental noise sources. Coffman rented a sound intensity probe, calibrated the probe, and conducted the measurements. The intensity measurements worked out as an effective solution to the high background noise levels from the surrounding environment.
