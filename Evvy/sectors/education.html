Education

Across all ages and educational institutions, the learning environment is an important space to have high quality acoustics. For the teacher who strains to be heard at the back row, the classroom can support clear speech for all students. Gymnasiums are regularly used for assemblies in many schools, and this can lead to poor sound for the audience during those gatherings.

Our services include, but are not limited to:
  • Room acoustics for auditorium, classrooms, bandrooms, and gyms
  • Sound isolation design between classrooms, especially where music practice takes place
  • Mechanical noise control so the air conditioning system does prevent some students from hearing instructions
