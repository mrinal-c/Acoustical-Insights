Healthcare

With HIPPA regulations and privacy an important concern for new and remodeled healthcare facilities, acoustical consulting can improve the experience for both medical professionals and patients. Acoustical Insights has experience working on sound isolation issues between patient rooms, mechanical noise from building equipment, and room acoustics where auditorium are part of a healthcare campus.

Our services include, but are not limited to:
  • MRI and CT machine noise and vibration control
  • HIPPA privacy using sound isolation design or sound masking systems
  • Mechanical Noise from building systems
  • Exterior to Interior noise control where transportation source exist near proposal medical facilties
  • Helipad design for noise and vibration
