Christopher Barnobi

Christopher has more than 15 years of experience in applied acoustical engineering. His work has included the analysis of sound generating sources, assessments of community and occupational noise levels, quantification of vibration levels from industrial equipment, and design input for interior space acoustical performance. He has conducted sound level measurements and modeling for a variety of sectors.
